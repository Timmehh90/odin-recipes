Caprese

Description

Ingredients

  • 8 Tomatoes
  • 4 Lasagna sheets
  • 400gr minced meat
  • 3 Onions

Steps

  1. Fry the minced meat and the chopped onions.
  2. Squish the tomatoes and add them to the pan.
  3. Layer the sauce in the oven pan, divide each layer with a lasagna sheet.
  4. Wait until the oven is done and Bona Petite!
